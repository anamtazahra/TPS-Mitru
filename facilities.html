Our Facilities

Laboratories

School laboratories provide hands-on experience for students, equipped with modern instruments and safety features, to explore scientific concepts and develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Health Care

Schools provide healthcare facilities, including clinics, first aid rooms, and medical staff, to ensure students' physical and mental well-being, and to provide immediate medical attention in case of emergencies.

Classrooms

Classrooms are equipped with facilities like comfortable seating, modern teaching aids, internet connectivity, proper lighting and ventilation, storage space, and audio-visual equipment, creating an ideal learning environment.

Security

Schools have robust security facilities, including CCTV cameras, secure entry/exit points, trained security personnel, emergency response systems, and regular drills to ensure students' safety and well-being.
