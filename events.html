Culture Day

Culture Day is a celebration of the diverse traditions, customs, and heritage that make up our community. It is a day to showcase our colorful and vibrant cultural expressions.

Defence Day

Defence Day commemorates the bravery and sacrifices of our armed forces in defending our nation's sovereignty. It is a tribute to their patriotism and courage.

14 August Independence Day

14 August marks the day of our nation's independence. It is a time to celebrate our freedom, reflect on our history, and honor the struggles of those who fought for our liberty.
